About Me

Who I Am

Hi, I’m Sysen Arlo Codell. I’m 21 and I love creating content that’s fun, creative, and entertaining. I enjoy doing lets plays, IRL streams, recording, and making content that brings enjoyment to my audience.

I’ve been inspired by many different content creators over the years, and that inspiration pushed me to develop my own style of creation.

My Journey

I started creating content because I loved the idea of making people smile and giving them something fun to watch. Over time, I experimented with streaming, recording, editing, and building my own creative style.

Every year I’ve improved my setup, learned new editing techniques, and pushed myself to make better content. My journey is all about growth, creativity, and connecting with people who enjoy what I create.
